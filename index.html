Background

Established in 2016, Orbit Media has been and will be a part of the industry in Malawi aiming to provide quality and crisp graphics to serious customers in act to promote the tredition of quality service for those who do not conform to the practice or are unaware. Back in 2016 when the company was still under the name of Orbit Graphics, the main point was to promote clean artwork graphics for start up musical artists who were missing an artwork for their music projects. Due to the outburst of artists in the country who want to monopolize their work, Orbit Graphics had to switch up to Orbit Media to take on newer markets and clients to benefit more than one set of consumers along with different products.
